HPE GreenLake Knowledge Base

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to add a service subscription to workspace?
Answer:
1. Login to GreenLake Cloud.
2. Click Global Navigation Menu.
3. Navigate to Services → Manage Service Subscription.
4. Click Add Service Subscription.
5. Enter the subscription key received via email.
6. Click Next.
7. Assign tags if required (optional).
8. Manage showback rates if required (optional).
9. Review details.
10. Click Finish.

Prerequisites:
- User must have Workspace Administrator role or permission to modify subscriptions.
- Services must be set up before adding subscription.
- Subscription key must be received from HPE.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to view service subscriptions?
Answer:
1. Go to Services → Manage Service Subscription.
2. View subscription status, expiration date, tier, and type.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to remove a service subscription?
Answer:
1. Go to Services → Manage Service Subscription.
2. Select the subscription.
3. Click Actions → Remove Subscriptions.
4. Confirm removal.

Note:
- Workspace Administrator role required.
- Tags are removed after deletion.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to convert workspace to MSP mode?
Answer:
1. Login as workspace administrator.
2. Navigate to Workspace Menu → Manage Workspace.
3. Click Manage Workspace Type.
4. Check eligibility.
5. Remove assignments and subscriptions from devices.
6. Remove unsupported services.
7. Click "Convert to MSP mode" button.
8. Click "Yes, Convert Now" button.
9. Workspace becomes temporarily unavailable during conversion.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to disable MSP mode?
Answer:
1. Login as workspace administrator.
2. Go to Manage Workspace → Manage Workspace Type.
3. Review and delete tenant workspaces.
4. Click "Convert to Standard Enterprise Mode".
5. Click "Yes, Convert Now".

Question: How to reset password in GreenLake?
Answer:
1. Go to https://greenlake.cloud/login
2. Click "Forgot Password".
3. Enter registered email address.
4. Click reset link from email.
5. Create new password with:
   - Minimum 12 characters
   - One uppercase letter
   - One lowercase letter
   - One number
   - One special character
6. Submit and login.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to invite users to workspace?
Answer:
1. Go to Manage Workspace.
2. Open Workspace Identity & Access.
3. Click Invite Users.
4. Send sign-up link.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to delete a user?
Answer:
1. Go to Workspace Identity & Access → Users.
2. Select user.
3. Click Delete.
4. Confirm deletion.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to view audit logs?
Answer:
1. Go to Manage Workspace.
2. Click Audit Log.
3. View login, logout, and activity details.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to create user roles?
Answer:
1. Go to Workspace Identity & Access → Roles & Permissions.
2. Click Create Role.
3. Select service and permissions.
4. Review and finish.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to add devices using CSV?
Answer:
1. Navigate to Devices → Devices Inventory.
2. Click Add Devices.
3. Upload CSV file with device details.
4. Assign tags if required.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to assign devices to a service?
Answer:
1. Go to Devices Inventory.
2. Select devices.
3. Click Assign to Service.
4. Select workspace and service instance.

--------------------------------------------------
Question: How to remove device subscription from service?
Answer:
1. Go to Devices Inventory → Assigned & Subscribed.
2. Select device.
3. Click Remove Assignment.
